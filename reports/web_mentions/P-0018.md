## Discrepancies

- **Birth year almost certainly wrong in GPKG:** The GPKG records Permelia's birth year as **1788**, but:
  1. The Maine Genealogy Archives death record gives her age at death as **78 in January 1845**, implying birth **c. 1767**.
  2. She married Sherebiah Lambert Jr. on **July 20, 1787** — she could not have been born in 1788 and married in 1787.
  3. The best FS candidate ("Permelia 'Millie' Oaks", LZNM-W98) was born **1768** in Harvard, Massachusetts.
  - All three lines of evidence converge on a birth year of approximately **1767–1768**, not 1788. This appears to be a GPKG data entry error. [https://archives.mainegenealogy.net/2010/03/deaths-in-norridgewock-canaan-and.html] [https://ancestors.familysearch.org/en/LDF8-39B/sherebiah-lambert-jr]
